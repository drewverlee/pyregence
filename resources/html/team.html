Pyregence Team

About Us

Pyregence consortium is founded on open science and technology principles, employing inclusivity and collaboration to tackle complex challenges.

Consortium partners are from leading research institutions from industry, academia, and government.

Workgroups

Extreme Weather
Workgroup #1

Determining weather conditions and other factors associated with large wildfires in the past

Fire Behavior
Workgroup #2

Advancing research on fuel mapping and fire spread to inform next generation wildfire models

Forecast Tools
Workgroup #3

Forecasting wildfire activity in California to inform the operation of a safe and reliable electric grid

Scenario Analyses
Workgroup #4

Analyzing wildfire scenarios under climate change to inform planning for a resilient electric grid

Project Administration Team

Overseeing and performing project administrative functions.

User Engagement Team

Identifying the needs of stakeholders to ensure project outputs can be operationalized and useful to end-users.

Project Leadership Team

Ensuring coordinated and timely delivery of project outcomes.

Integration Team

Advising and facilitating the integration of findings and products across project workgroups and externally with other research initiatives.

Technical Advisory Committee

An independent advisory committee composed of professionals from utilities, state and federal research and emergency management agencies charged with providing project guidance and advisory product review.

Lead Project Investigator

Spatial Informatics Group and University of San Francisco

Project Principal Investigator, Spatial Informatics Group - Dr. Saah has the responsibility for oversight of the project. Dr. Saah is committed to producing high quality research projects that integrate the most current science and technology. He is dedicated to the accurate dissemination of results from these endeavors through innovative presentations, publications, and workshops.

Leroy Westerling, PhD

Professor of Management of Complex Systems

UC Merced
