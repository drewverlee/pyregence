State of Wildfire Science Report: Limitations in Modelling Modern Wildfire Behavior

Download

This report provides a targeted critique of operational wildfire models. The central thesis is that a gap in wildfire science, namely the absence of a physical theory of wildland fire spread, constrains the ability of existing models to accurately represent fire behavior in many of California's contemporary wooded landscapes. The report briefly describes the modeling framework necessary for assessing fire risk. Submitted January 17, 2020.
